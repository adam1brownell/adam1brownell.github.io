What is Pingd?

Our Mission Statement

The secret to a meaningful life is meaningful relationships.

•

Pingd is here to help you keep in touch with every corner of your network.

•

We believe connection, community, and growth happen through personal outreach - not just by liking someone’s photo. 80% of maintaining a relationship is simply staying in touch.

•

And we get it, life is busy. Remembering to grab coffee with your old college roommate, or email networking contacts isn’t always easy.

•

That’s why we created Pingd, the only app designed to help you stay in touch with who you want, how you want through convenient, personalized reminders that are customizable to you.
